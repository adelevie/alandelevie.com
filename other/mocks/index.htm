The first paragraph of a small, title-less post. In this I ruminate on a short subject or something and then carry on with my life. It will involve one, two, or possibly ten paragraphs, the point is that it's not a canonical thing that I expect other people to visit - but, it will have a permalink and all that just in case.

A title will still be entered into the database and a slug generated, but the title will not be shown, although it will be present in the HTML (just marked as invisible). The slug and title will be utilitarian and descriptive.
